About

I received my Bachelor of Science and Engineering from Fukushima University in March 2023, and my Master of Science and Engineering from Fukushima University in March 2025.
During my undergraduate and master's studies, I was engaged in research on communication robots, acquiring a wide range of skills from hardware to software.
Currently, at the Cybernetics and Reality Engineering Laboratory at the Nara Institute of Science and Technology, I am working on the research and development of a color identification support interface for people with color vision deficiency, motivated by my own personal challenges with the condition.
